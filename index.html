Health

Mosquitos have given humans a hard time being carriers of diseases like Dengue and Zika! We have used the Nasa Hydrosphere dataset to predict the habitat map of mosquitos and the potential threat they pose

Forest Cover

When analyzing data from Nasa's data we found that apart from urbanisation, one of the major causes of preventable deforestation are forest fires! So, we have used forest cover data to predict the extent of damage and then some preventive measures that help prevent them.

Solar

Solar energy has always been an underused energy resource. We have used cloud cover, precipitation and water vapour cover to predict the efficiency of solar panels. The calculator helps people calculate their daily requirements and enable them to switch to renewable sources

Crop Yield

Visualizing countries based on their maximum cash crop production capacity. The highest ones being corn, rice, wheat and potatoes
