Life is precious!

Over one million deaths occur every year due to dengue and Malaria, spread by mosquitos. Recently, the Zika virus has resulted in severe birth defects and life threatning diseases, primarily spread by mosquito bites.

Prevention Measures:
1. Prevent accumulation of stagnant water near yourself.
2. Use mosquito repellants.
3. Don't go out in the late evenings.
4. Maintain cleanliness.
